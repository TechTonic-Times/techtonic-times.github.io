Article Guidelines

Help us maintain quality by following these editorial standards.

  • Original content only — no plagiarism or duplicate submissions
  • Clear, accurate, and fact-based information
  • Credible sources preferred — link to research when possible
  • Reader-friendly formatting — short paragraphs, clear headings
  • Engaging headlines that accurately reflect content
  • Mobile readability — avoid overly complex formatting
  • Future-focused perspective encouraged
Recommended length: 800–2000+ words
Writing style: Clear, informative, professional, accessible to general readers
